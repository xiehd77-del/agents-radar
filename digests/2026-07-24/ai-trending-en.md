## Trend Signal Analysis

**Agent infrastructure is the dominant narrative** — the two largest projects by star count (ECC at 232K, hermes-agent at 219K) are agent harness systems, and trending today shows strong activity around tool integrations like OmniRoute (+1,929 today), which abstracts away provider complexity. Developers are clearly moving from "which model" to "how to orchestrate agents reliably."

**A new paradigm may be emerging in knowledge representation.** Graphify-Labs/graphify (94K stars) explicitly positions itself as "no vector store" — using deterministic AST parsing and knowledge graphs instead. Combined with cognee (29K stars, self-hosted knowledge graph memory) and LEANN (97% storage savings without vectors), the community appears to be exploring **post-vector-database RAG**. This could signal a shift away from embedding-based retrieval toward structured, causal knowledge representation for agents.

**Financial AI is a breakout vertical.** Three major projects target finance: Kronos (foundation model, +401 today trending), TradingAgents (94K stars, multi-agent trading framework), and Vibe-Trading (26K stars). The combination of LLM reasoning with quantitative trading logic — especially multi-agent architectures — is attracting both developer and financial institution attention.

**Edge/novel sensing is emerging.** RuView (+1,708 today) uses commodity WiFi for spatial intelligence and vital sign monitoring — no cameras needed. This represents a new class of AI-powered sensing that bypasses privacy concerns of video surveillance while enabling ambient intelligence.

**The "one gateway to rule them all" pattern is accelerating.** OmniRoute's 290+ providers, 500+ models, auto-fallback, and 15-95% token compression reflects growing fatigue with provider lock-in. The community wants abstraction layers that make switching models trivial — a likely prerequisite for production agent deployments.
